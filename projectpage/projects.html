Project Summary:

The objective of this project is to develop a digital platform that enables students to engage in peer-to-peer transactions, including the exchange, lending, and retrieval of lost items. The platform will encompass user registration, item posting, notifications, search and filtering capabilities, feedback mechanisms, and social media sharing. The team's mission is to successfully develop and launch the product in accordance with user needs and regulatory requirements.
